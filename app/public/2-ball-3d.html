        How to play 1v1 Battle?

        1v1Battle: Build Fight Simulator is an exciting third-person shooter in Battle Royale style in which you will have to train your skills as a warrior very hard if you want to defeat your opponents in dangerous fights.

        Improve your battle skills by training with all kinds of extremely dangerous firearms, and learn how to build a sturdy shelter or defensive turrets from which to raise your attack position as you move through an infinite landscape full of nooks and crannies to explore. As the game progresses and you win some battles, you'll have the opportunity to improve your shooting skills. Don't give up on the dangers that come your way and fight to survive until your last breath - there can only be one left!
